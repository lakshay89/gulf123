Your Trusted Partner in the Gulf Recruitment

G-International is a medical recruitment company recruiting Doctors, Nurses, and Other Medical Staffs to the Gulf Countries.

An ISO 9001:2015 certified company, G-International is headquartered at New Delhi, exclusively working for the healthcare sector including Doctors, Nurses, and Health Professionals. Our company is incorporated with the Government of India under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs.

With tremendous experience in sourcing and deploying the most competent healthcare professionals, G-International caters to internationally reputed clients across all spheres.

We specialize in identifying, evaluating, and placing the right candidates to precisely meet our clients' requirements. Our workforce spread over India has one mission: to find the right candidate for each client’s specific needs.

Our client relationships are based on trust, discretion, and confidentiality. We are fully focused and dedicated to the long-term success of both our clients and candidates without compromising their careers.

Prometric Exam Registration

Simplify Your Journey to Completing Exams with G-International Recruitment Consultancy!

Are you aiming to pass a Prometric exam to secure a prominent healthcare job in the Gulf? We help you make hassle-free registrations whenever it suits you.

Registering for these exams can be tricky, but don't worry! Our complete assistance ensures your registration process is smooth, letting you focus on acing your exam.
